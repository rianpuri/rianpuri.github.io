Salaam Baalak Trust (SBT) (2018-22)
SBT is a nonprofit organization set up in 1988 to support street children in Delhi. SBT runs shelters
and literacy programs and has helped over 120,000 children since its inception.

  • Volunteered to teach students of ages 10 - 14 years concepts of math and English
  • Designed and conducted computer skills classes to help train students to navigate basic programs
    on a computer.

Residents Welfare Association (RWA) Defence Colony (2020-21)
  • Led a team of volunteers to find and verify leads on social media for sourcing oxygen cylinders,
    plasma, hospital beds, and medicines for people in need.
  • Set up an Emergency Hotline and online notice board for colony residents during the 2nd wave
    of the pandemic.
  • Created a directory of stores delivering medicines and other essentials.
  • Organized a food collection drive and helped set up donation centers for daily-wage workers
    during the lockdown.

Smartphone Users Workshop for Senior Citizens - Arya Samaj Center (2019)
  • Conducted two workshops to help senior citizens in my community better use their smartphones
    and computers.
